Our CEO – A.K.Amara

Hi, I’m Amara, the CEO and founder of Sparkle Beauty Salon. I completed my MBA in Cosmetology from Birmingham City University, London. My professional journey began at a leading beauty salon, where I spent three years learning the finest beauty techniques and insider knowledge from experienced mentors. The guidance I received from my seniors during that time is something I cherish deeply. Their training and support have been instrumental in shaping my career, and I am forever grateful for their encouragement.

In 2015, I decided to take the leap and open my own salon, Sparkle, realizing a long-held dream. It took a year of hard work and dedication to build Sparkle into the space I had envisioned. I was fortunate to have my mentors by my side, helping me every step of the way. Their belief in me fueled my determination to create a salon that focuses on personalized care, professional expertise, and client satisfaction.

At Sparkle, nothing brings me more joy than seeing the smiles on our clients' faces. It’s those moments of happiness that make all the hard work worth it. I believe in creating beauty experiences that leave a lasting positive impact, and I am committed to ensuring that every client walks out feeling confident and radiant. Sparkle is not just a beauty salon; it’s a place where every client’s uniqueness is celebrated.

About Sparkle Beauty Salon

Sparkle is renowned as one of the best beauty salons in the world, offering top-tier services across various countries. Our main branch is based in India, with additional branches in London, the USA, Thailand, South Korea, and Japan. We are proud to provide on-demand doorstep services at no extra charge, allowing our clients to experience premium beauty care without the need to visit the salon. However, certain services may require a visit to one of our salons, and we offer the convenience of booking an appointment in advance to ensure a personalized experience at your preferred location.

At Sparkle, we believe in nurturing not just beauty but also talent. We offer comprehensive training programs for aspiring makeup artists, complete with professional certification to help them advance in their careers. Our services cover a wide spectrum, from haircuts and spa treatments to advanced makeup for high-profile clients, including celebrities from Hollywood, Tollywood, Bollywood, and beyond.

We are committed to using only natural, high-quality products because we prioritize the health and satisfaction of our clients. Your beauty, well-being, and confidence are at the heart of everything we do. Whether it’s a celebrity makeover or a casual spa day, we promise you’ll leave Sparkle feeling radiant and rejuvenated. Trust us to enhance your natural beauty—you won’t regret choosing Sparkle for all your beauty needs.

The Sparkle Family

At Sparkle Beauty Salon, our team is made up of highly skilled and passionate professionals dedicated to delivering exceptional beauty services. Each member brings years of experience and specialized expertise in their respective fields, from hair and makeup to spa treatments and nail art. We believe in continuous learning, ensuring our staff stays up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques to provide you with the best possible results. Our team’s commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction is what makes Sparkle stand out. Together, we strive to create an environment where every client feels valued, pampered, and more confident than ever.
